About
—

I was born and raised in Johannesburg, South Africa. I have been married for over 20 years and have two grown children. I have lived in Johns Creek, Georgia since 1996.

I am a health and wellness consultant and an avid fitness professional with certifications in Personal Training, Spin, and Bodyworks. However, my true passion is Yoga and Ayurveda. By creating amazing moments of profound energy, my world has changed forever. The inspirational and empowering feel for yoga is what I want to share with my students. For over a decade, I have been helping clients find physical, mental, and spiritual balance.

I believe that we use the physical intensity of the practice to heal and tone the body as well to challenge and expand our understanding of ourselves. My goal as an instructor is to motivate and inspire others to use yoga as physical exercise and as a mind-body connection that can deepen consciousness and tranquility. Believing that yoga is a unique experience for everyone, I have found my Dharma in teaching the healing principles of yoga, so that everyone can find balance, peace, and love.

Classes
—

Beginners Yoga Series
—

Interested in yoga, but unsure where to start? This class is designed as an introduction to the practice of yoga.

Basics
—

Explore some of the basic postures, learn proper alignment, core strength, flexibility and balance, as well as concentration and breath control.

All Levels
—

In this class we focus on posture and alignment to promote flexibility and strength in the body. Breathing techniques and meditation are also integrated.

Gentle
—

Suitable for everyone. A restful, calming class including breathing, gentle movements, passive and supported poses. This class is also appropriate for those working with injury, limited mobility or who prefer a softer approach to yoga.

Power Flow
—

These classes are designed to cultivate heat in the body through creative sequences involving sun salutations, standing & seated postures and a strong focus on the power of breath awareness.

Yoga For Bone Strength
—

Whether you suffer from Osteoporosis, Osteopenia, Arthritis, or just want to practice yoga in a safe environment, this is the perfect class for you.
